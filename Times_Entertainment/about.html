Q & A

What is Times Entertainment?

Times Entertainment is a free online website that offer various suggestions of passing time while being entertained. Currently we have three options: watching, reading and playing. More content are expected to come soon as time passes.

How is the content decided?

The list of recommendations offered by this website is being updated on a running basis. Popular content from the current month will be added to the current list on the website. If something that is desired is not listed, please feel free to fill out this form here.

Are there going to be more forms of entertainment?

Yes, we are currently working on a music section. We will annouce updates along the way on our Patreon page. Subscribe to our page for the latest updates and news for Times Entertainment here for free. We are always open to new ideas!

Is there anyway to save my searching/exploring process?

Our devs are working on a solution. Currently, beta tests are in process. We are doing finshing touchs to make sure account creation is secure and data for each account is properly saved. Until then, users can try using the 'Ctrl + D' keys to bookmark the desired page(s). For additional help click here.
