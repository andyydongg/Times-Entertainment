Privacy Policy

Introduction

The times entertainment is in the very early stages of development. Currently, the website does NOT save, copy or steal any information from your device. The only information that is extracted from your device is the time, which is used to display the running clock (upper right hand corner all pages), this is information is also not saved anywhere.


Account Registration/Login

There is no current option to login to save your progress of exploring our website. This part is under development. What we have confirmed so far is: the website will use cookies to store information regarding your personal account. There will be also be a 15 minute wait time for personal login information to be synced across devices. Accounts made on the website will be protected through MFA (Multi-factor Authentication). Accounts deleted will be able to restore within a three month period, anything outside of that time range will be out of our hands.


External Sources/Links

All sources linked on this website is external sources. The quality will be not too great at times, so you can choose to purchase the same thing through online stores. When purchasing items, if something sounds too good to be true it IS too good to be true. Sometimes some links will lead to a PDF file, you can try using the adobe PDF reader for better readability. Some sources on this website links to free resources, but will have a lot of ads and popups. Do not click on the popups as it might be a scam, to aviod this consider install a adblock.
